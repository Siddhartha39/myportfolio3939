About Us

Building India's Industrial Future

Jindal Steel Ltd. (officially since July 2025) is one of India's leading integrated steel producers, operating under the prestigious O.P. Jindal Group. Headquartered in New Delhi, we are a major global player in steel, mining, and infrastructure.

With significant operations across India and Africa, we continue to drive innovation in steel manufacturing while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and excellence.
